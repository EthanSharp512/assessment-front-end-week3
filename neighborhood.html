My Neighborhood

Click play for accompanying music

I recently moved to Daybreak in South Jordan, Utah, and here is a little bit about my neighborhood.

My neighborhood is relatively quiet and very condensed with how all the homes are so close together. It is really nice here, but it seems like the people can be a little bit snobby, but that is probably just me having preconceived judgements, having never lived in an community like this before. Overall, it is a good place to live and here is some information about what it has to offer.

Resturants

  • The Break - Sports Bar & Grill
  • Cupbop - Korean BBQ
  • Tio's Restaurant - Mexican Food

Parks

  • Brookside Park - Playground
  • Ewok Park - Hammock Park
  • West Beach Park - Lake Side Trail
  • Daybreak BMX Park - Bike Trails and Jumps
